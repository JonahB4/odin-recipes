Pizza Dish

Description

This mouthwatering pizza dish is a favorite among pizza enthusiasts. It features a crispy crust topped with a flavorful sauce, melted cheese, and various toppings, creating a delightful pizza experience.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., vegetables, meats, herbs)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to the recommended temperature for the pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle cheese generously over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings, distributing them evenly.
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and slice
