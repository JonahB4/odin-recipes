Pasta Dish

Description

This delicious pasta dish is a classic recipe that never disappoints. It features al dente pasta tossed in a flavorful sauce, creating a satisfying meal for pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Protein (optional)
  • Herbs and spices
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the sauce by saut√©ing vegetables and adding desired seasonings.
  3. If using protein, cook it separately and add it to the sauce.
  4. Drain the cooked pasta and combine it with the sauce.
  5. Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with cheese and herbs, if desired.
